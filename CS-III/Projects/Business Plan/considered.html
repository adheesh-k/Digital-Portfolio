Programming Language Converter

Business Plan

Overall, I think this idea is a great concept. Converting from one programming language to another would be very useful, as many programmers will not be proficent in all programming languages. However, I do not believe that this idea is feasible to implement, as each major programming language has its own purposes. There will only be a few scenarios where one programming languages can be converted into another, and these will mostly be simple problems where not much code is required.

"SchoolFood" App

Business Plan

This idea is for an app that allows students to order their lunch from the school cafeteria online. The main goal of this app is to minimize long lines and wait times in school cafeterias, which is especially prevalent in our school. However, I believe that this will not be easy to implement, as school cafeterias can sometimes be under-staffed, and the delivery of food may take extensive amounts of time. Moreover, it will be difficult to convince many schools to adopt this system, as they will have to uproot their existing system in order to switch to this new one.

Social Media Bot

Business Plan

This product is a bot that goes through data from social media platforms in order to help a business gain a better understanding of their target audience and work towards better consumer interaction. In my opinion, social media platforms are an excellent platform for companies to advertise their products and reach their target audiences. However, many social media platforms already have an existing methods of connecting businesses to their consumers, and problems regarding ethicality could arise through using the data of users without their consent.

"PlaceHolder"

Business Plan

This ISP allows for privacy with phone and computer screens, where it will automatically shut off if someone besides the user is looking at the screen. I believe that privacy on our devices is crucial. Especially today, we keep a lot of crucial information on our phones, laptops, etc. which we do not want leaked to others. However, I believe that this product can have a lot of bugs, there will many many cases where the product can be accidentally triggerd and accidentally shut off the screen. Moreover, this problem can be solved by using a privacy screen protector.

Automated Window Shades (Selected)

Business Plan

This product automates window shades with the goal of saving energy consumption in homes. I have chosen this ISP because it is both real-world applicable and easy to implement. Many homeowners would want to save money on their electricity bill, and environmental organizations would want to reduce the consumption of energy, especially through non-renewable sources. The automation of window shades could help with the regulation of temperature in homes, as sunlight can warm up homes through windows. This will be relatively easy to implement, as the main computer is a Raspberry Pi connected to a motor which controlls existing window shades. The computer will use sensors for sunlight and indoor temperature, as well as APIs for weather in order to determine the optimal amount of sunlight coming through the window shades.
